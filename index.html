Q) How do I avail this service for my company?
+

A) In order to avail this service, you have to be a Corporate or SME customer of Robi. Once signed up as a Corporate or SME customer, please talk to your account manager who will create your user account in the system and help you with the initial setup of the service.

Q) Do I need to be a postpaid or prepaid customer to avail this service?
+

A) You or your employees who will be assigned as call recipients can be either prepaid or postpaid Robi users but the Robi number that will be used as the Single number for incoming and outgoing calls has to be a postpaid number.

Q) Can I pick any postpaid number as the Single Robi number?
+

A) The Single Robi number is a virtual postpaid number that is configured in the system. When you sign up for the service, you will be given a choice to select from a list of pre-configured postpaid numbers to set as your Single Robi number.

Q) What happens if all the recipients in my company are busy and a customer calls?
+

A) If a customer calls and all the recipients are busy attending other calls, you can select the option for the customer to hear a hold tune for a certain time after which they will be asked if they want to record a message and place a call-back request. If the customer leaves a call back request, the Mobile Business Connect will store this and make an automated outbound call to an available recipient and play the message followed by a prompt to make an outgoing call back to the customer that left the voice message.

Q) Where can I setup and manage the recipients and IVR tree for my business?
+

A) When you sign up for the service, your account manager will create a user account for you or anyone in your company that you designate as the admin in Mobile Business Connect platform. This is a web based portal where the admin will login using the user credentials. This is a self-service portal which can also be used for setting up bulk message broadcasts.

Q) Do I have to setup an IVR against the Single Robi number?
+

A) You can setup the IVR for your Single Robi number in the MBC platform depending on your requirements. This does not mean that you have to have an IVR setup for your Single Robi number. You can choose to skip the IVR and let your customers hear the ringing tone until they are connected to an available recipient.

Q) Will the incoming calls from the customers be routed to all the recipients or will the − system check each available recipient according to a sequence?
+

A13) The incoming call from a customer will be handled by the system and routed to an available recipient after checking which recipient is free to take the call. The checking can be done by the system according to a fixed sequence or a random sequence which can be set in the MBC portal.

Q) Can I set the availability hours of the recipients to receive the calls from the customers?
+

A) Yes, the availability of the recipients can be set in the MBC portal. The recipients can be routed calls from the customers only during their availability hours. Beyond availability hours recipients can make and receive calls to their friends, families and others just like a normal mobile number.

Q) What happens if customers call after the business hours and none of the recipients − are available?
+

A) You can set different greeting tones for customers within and beyond business hours in the MBC portal. If customers call after business hours, your greeting tone can inform them that current time is under non-business hours and when you will be next available to receive the call.

Can one recipient call another recipient?
+

A) Yes, one recipient can call another recipient or any other person using his number. This will be treated as a regular mobile call and will be charged to the recipient according to CUG, On-net or Off-net rates whichever is applicable.

Q) Can a recipient receive calls from anyone other than a customer during the availability − hours?
+

A) Yes, the recipient can receive calls from anyone other than a customer during availability hours provided he is not engaged in another call. This is the beauty of Robi’s Mobile Business Connect. If the recipient receives this call, the system will find him as unavailable when trying to connect a customer to a recipient.
